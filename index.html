Length: 154 chars, includes CTAs and USPs

Heading Structure (H1-H6)

Current Issues:

• Missing H1 tags on 12 product pages
• Multiple H1s on category pages
• No keyword optimisation in headings

Recommended Structure:

H1: One per page, includes primary keyword
H2: Section headers with secondary keywords
H3-H6: Logical hierarchy for better user experience

Internal Linking Strategy

  • Contextual Links: Add 3-5 internal links per page using keyword-rich anchor text
  • Breadcrumbs: Implement schema-marked breadcrumbs on all pages
  • Related Products: Dynamic "You might also like" sections
  • Content Hubs: Create topic clusters around "Gluten-Free", "Quick Dinners", "BBQ"
